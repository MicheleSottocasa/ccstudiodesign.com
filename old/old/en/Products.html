Dyed-yarn designs

CCStudio thirty-year experience in shirt designs guarantees high quality and competence in this field. We offer our know-how and savoir faire. Developing original designs and colorways, new collections and exclusive ideas. Of course every designs has its own technical information, ready to be woven.

Jacquard

With an expert and efficient team of designers we can develop different kind of Jacquard designs. With our Jacquard CAD we can easily satisfy your needs and create your exclusive fabrics. Working with different kind of yarn and structure we can offer a wide set of designs.

Prints

Printing is a world to explore. The art of coloring and choosing the right fabric background make us able to offer thousand of possible combinations. Ready to be printed out our designs can be modified according with costumers taste.

Some ideas

With our innovative design system we can develop also designs for socks and underwear, projecting designs and giving our customers also high-quality preview of their final products.

3D simulation

We also able to apply our designs to flat image and create fantastic cloth simulation. Necktie, bow tie, shirt, swimwear and more. Furthermore we can even simulate 3D items.
