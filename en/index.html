We design and sell textile designs to fashion brand and weaving mills

We are specialized in developing print designs and dyed-yarn design proposing innovative styles and custom-made solutions.
Tartans or classic stripes, conversational prints or geometric, dobbies or jacquards, our designs are made to complete textile and fashion collections all around the world.
Techincally made and carefully developed, our designs delight both designers and buyers, making us able to safisfy every kind of need, enhancing your brand identity.



We have a three-generation identity

Our family met the textile world more than 70 years ago, and since then we felt in love with this beautiful world.
Our company has a family-business mindset and we are happy to say that we are the third generation of textile designers. A passion and a kind of art that we keep alive in time thorugh our designs and archives. Tradition and innovation make a winning duo.



We believe in a global sense of collaboration

We make our technical and creative skills completely available for all our customers.
Our distribution sales network is growing and we are always travelling in order to be in the right place at the right time. This mindset helps us to have a global view.
We actively partecipate in tradeshows and with our online Shop we are always close to our customers.
